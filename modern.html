Modern Room

Modern design is a style of interior design that emerged in the early twentieth century and continues to inspire contemporary interior designers. It is characterized by clean and simple color schemes, welcoming feel, clean individual elements, as well as heavy use of glass and steel. Black and white dominate the color palette, with some faded shades of blue, red, among other prime colors.

Project Overview

The tasks were quite practical and understandable. It was necessary to create a comfortable place for the whole family to socialize, storage spaces, and bathrooms. We had to make separate bedrooms for the children, a parent's bedroom, a separate parent's bathroom. A large living room and a large appliance-filled kitchen were also desired.

Design Process

So, the customers got "bare walls" in a house built in 2006. Concrete without finishing, so they did not have to overpay for repairs in the aesthetic preferences of the previous owners, which is already good. Except that there were rough partitions, we had to dismantle them.

The layout is comfortable and logical. The hallway with a fenced "dirty zone" - the dirt will not spread throughout the house. There is a second bathroom at the entrance, which is also a plus. Then the exit directly to the kitchen-living room, and only after that - bedrooms. It is great when guests do not see private rooms.
