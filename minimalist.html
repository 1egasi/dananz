Minimalist Room

Minimalism is a design style that prioritizes the essential. It is characterized by the use of limited materials, neutral colors, simple forms, and avoiding excess ornamentation to achieve a pure form of elegance. Minimalist design can be found in architecture, interiors, art, graphics, fashion, and virtually every other facet of design.

Project Overview

The customers wanted to make the interior minimalist. They wanted their new home to look minimalist, but not boring. For more dissolution of furniture we used a retractable system, which closes the TV, for the time when it is not needed. The main feature of the interior is the music zone, which is located in the center of the apartment and can be separated by glass partitions. Inside the music zone there is noise insulation, special lighting and everything you need to do your favorite thing without disturbing others.

Design Process

Also interesting is the solution with the sofa, which faces the TV with one part and the dining table with the other. Above the dining table, we placed an accent lamp, hypertrophied in size. The kitchen, by combining it with the living room, has a lot of working areas, as well as a bar island.

In addition to separate bathrooms, the apartment has a laundry room with large storage systems. One of the bathrooms is equipped with a bathtub, and the second bathroom has a shower cabin. The bedroom is in dark, muted colors. In order to make a small workplace, we joined the loggia.
