Vintage Room

Vintage design refers to an item of another era that holds important and recognizable value. This style can be applied to interior design, decor, clothing and other areas The term “vintage” is used to describe an item that is between 40 and 100 years old, made of high-quality materials and/or craftsmanship, and characteristic of a specific time period or artist.

Project Overview

Pleasant, intelligent people approached our studio to develop a design project. The customers wanted to see their home bright and cozy. Not too overloaded and cluttered, so that there was inspiring space and freedom. In short, the designer had to develop a project for a comfortable and well-thought-out apartment for permanent residence for a family with a child.

September 12 - 2022
Interior Design - Planning

Design Process

Since the apartment has two floors, it was divided by floor. The entire first floor is given over to the so-called public area, where friends and guests come, where a courier or a doctor can drop by. On the second level there are private areas for sleeping, rest and sports. The apartment also has a non-standard layout and high mansard ceilings, which, of course, played its part.

The apartment turned out to be very atmospheric and modern. Not the least role here is played by the choice of such materials as wood and stone. We hope that this apartment will become a cozy and reliable home for a wonderful family.
