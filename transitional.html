Transitional Room

Transitional design is a style of interior design that combines traditional and modern elements in a harmonious way. This style is characterized by the use of neutral color palettes, simple lines, and a focus on texture. Transitional design aims to create a space that is both comfortable and inviting, while also being sleek and modern.

Project Overview

The client of the project is a young ambitious girl, demanding in her work, but at the same time a very sensitive and warm-hearted person. She will be living in the apartment alone for the first time. But, of course, we immediately planned the possibility of transforming the apartment for a family and even a pet. We also planned the possibility of transforming the study into a nursery.

Interior Design - Planning

Design Process

There were a lot of variants in the planning, and everything was approached very carefully. The most important thing was to divide the apartment into a guest and private area and to make all the additional rooms for the client's needs.

It has a separate kitchen-living room, a hall and a guest bathroom, as well as a study. Now it is definitely convenient to receive guests here. And the personal space was separated into a private zone, which is the second part of the apartment: there is a walk-in dressing room, a master bedroom and a separate bathroom.
